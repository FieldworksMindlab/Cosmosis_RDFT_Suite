# App Overview: Cosmosis Visual Observatory

See `README.md` for full instructions.

This app is the visual-only RDFT laboratory. It does not require SuperCollider
or BasinNET. The Surface Foundry page generates STL, call-sheet, and
relief/cutout artifacts from the live topology field. Its opt-in DCRTE primitive
pipeline observes the unchanged field through analytic sphere, box, and
cylinder domains with hard-interior or inward shell-band constraints.

The DCRTE Milestone 4 scheduler can freeze that qualified candidate volume and
either activate it immediately or propagate a deterministic connected frontier
from Cartesian or intrinsic seed strategies. Scheduler batches are relational
ordering steps, not physical time, and the M4 framework contains no entropy
heuristic.

Milestone 5 adds an optional state-dependent entropic scheduler without
changing the frozen candidate mask. Milestone 6 then adds source-shell,
scheduled-scaffold, inset, anchor-bridge, and explicit support-material roles
between that scheduler state and the existing materializer. The imported SDF
remains the authoritative envelope, locked shell voxels are preserved, and
legacy `SCAFFOLD_ONLY` output remains the exact M5 path. A deterministic torus
fixture exercises the normal imported STL pipeline in Cartesian mode; the
existing closed-loop intrinsic safety gate remains active.

Geodesic Salon is the tenth visual workspace. It builds validated tetrahedral,
octahedral, icosahedral, dodecahedron-derived, and
rhombic-triacontahedron-derived domes or spheres with face-local barycentric
and center-to-edge coordinates. Tetrahedral, octahedral, and icosahedral
networks have an active radial face-stellation modifier. Its fabrication kit
uses a dedicated timestamped folder and emits one STL per reusable panel type,
a quantity-based bill of materials, a face-neighbor assembly plan, and one
audited combined shell. The optional `109.471` degree Plateau overlay is a
junction diagnostic and remains separate from the intrinsic coordinate
contract. Panel fabrication can emit a solid panel, an open perimeter frame,
or a frame with a thin center membrane. Matched fastener portals, reciprocal
half-dihedral miters, and corner clearances are generated from shared-edge
length and bubble-aware dihedral metadata. Shells are gated by topology,
boundary-chain, discrete Gauss-Bonnet, and manifold checks. Optional raised
mate codes are audited per reusable type and fall back to documented
BOM/JSON/guide codes if the emboss alone would invalidate an otherwise
fabricable panel.
