# Audio Instrumentation

The audio side of the suite uses SuperCollider and Processing as a coupled instrument. Processing computes and routes field, orbit, holonomy, surface, Floquet, environmental, and control states. SuperCollider receives those states over OSC and turns them into layered synthesis behavior.

Important mappings include:

- Field intensity and orbit motion shape timbre and event density.
- Floquet terms modulate periodic forcing and layer asymmetry.
- Solar, ocean, galaxy, and surface bridges act as live shaper inputs.
- Holonomy and Berry metrics support loop analysis and performance gestures.
- Surface/topology senders allow geometric state to influence audio behavior.

The synth systems are not just sound engines. They are sonification laboratories for testing how recursive field ideas feel, behave, and become performable.
